Impact Labs Series 1: (10 sessions)
Salento Apuglia Project - Our Impact Project will focus on working with local farmers in the Puglia region, whose olive farms have been devastated by Xilella Fastidiosa and need to be entirely replanted. We plan to develop and implement a ‘Responsible Holidays’ booking platform. This will allow tourists to identify and book responsible holidays – where families and corporate teams can help the local farmers to rebuild their farms, while lodging at the rustic Italian farms and experiencing the charm of southern Italy first hand.
